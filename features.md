# 🌾 Annadata AI - Feature List

Annadata AI is a comprehensive digital ecosystem designed to empower Indian farmers with intelligent tools and real-time data. Below are the key features of the platform:

## 🤖 AI Assistant (Your Smart Farming Advisor)
- **Multi-language Support**: Communicate in **Hindi, English**, and other regional languages.
- **Voice & Text Input**: Ask questions by typing or using voice commands for easier accessibility.
- **Deep Farming Knowledge**: Expert advice on crop recommendations, pest management, fertilizer schedules, and weather-based guidance.
- **24/7 Availability**: Get instant answers to farming queries anytime.

## 📚 Crop Intelligence (Library)
- **200+ Crop Varieties**: Detailed database including wheat, soybean, cotton, and more.
- **Regional Optimization**: Insights tailored to specific soil types and climates across India.
- **Actionable Guides**:
    - **Soil Requirements**: Best soil types for maximum yield.
    - **Irrigation Schedules**: Precise water needs and timing.
    - **Pest Management**: Identification and organic/chemical control methods.
    - **Fertilizer Recommendations**: Region-specific nutrient schedules.

## 📈 Market Intelligence (Live Mandi Rates)
- **Real-time Price Tracking**: Live commodity prices from mandis across districts.
- **Price Comparison**: Compare rates across different markets to find the best selling price.
- **Trend Analysis**: View price movements (7-day history) to make informed selling decisions.
- **Localized Data**: Filters by State and District for relevant local market info.

## 🤝 Community Q&A
- **Farmer-to-Expert Connection**: Get answers from agricultural experts.
- **Peer Knowledge Sharing**: Connect with fellow farmers to share experiences and best practices.
- **Topic-based Discussions**: Specific categories for **Crop Advisory, Pest Control, Irrigation, and Government Schemes**.
- **Multilingual Threads**: Post and read in your preferred language.

## 🚛 Transport & Logistics (Farm-to-Mandi)
- **Pickup Requests**: Simple form to request crop transportation.
- **Verified Providers**: Connect with trusted nearby transport services.
- **Delivery Tracking**: Real-time tracking of crop shipments from the farm to the destination.
- **Provider Directory**: Search and contact nearby logistics providers directly.

## 🔑 Technical & User Experience Features
- **Secure Authentication**: Robust login system powered by **Firebase Phone Authentication**.
- **Modern UI/UX**:
    - **Fully Responsive**: Optimized for mobile and desktop browsers.
    - **Stunning Aesthetics**: Premium dark theme with glassmorphism and smooth animations (Framer Motion).
    - **Interactive Elements**: Real-time counters, scroll reveals, and particle effects.
- **Full Multilingual Support**: Seamless toggle between **Hindi and English** interfaces.
- **Educational Resources**: Integrated video guides and step-by-step "How It Works" tutorials.

*Built with React, Vite, Node.js, and Firebase.*
